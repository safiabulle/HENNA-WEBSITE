# Henna by Safia

This is a simple website for **Safia**, a professional henna artist. The site shows her story, the services she offers, her henna designs, and allows clients to contact her.

## Project Overview
The Henna by Safia website is designed to showcase Safia's work, share her story, and allow clients to book appointments. The site has a clean, elegant aesthetic with henna-inspired colors and a user-friendly layout.

---

## Features
- **Homepage** with a banner and call-to-action button.
- **About Page** detailing Safia's story, services, and reasons to choose her.
- **Gallery Page** displaying henna designs using a responsive CSS Grid layout.
- **Contact Page** with a contact form and contact information.
- **Responsive Design** for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- Smooth hover effects and interactive buttons.

---

## Pages
1. **index.html** – Homepage with banner and tagline.
2. **about.html** – About page describing Safia, her story, services, and why choose her.
3. **gallery.html** – Gallery page showcasing various henna designs with images and descriptions.
4. **contact.html** – Contact page with a form to send messages and contact information.
